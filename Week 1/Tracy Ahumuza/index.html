Tracy Ahumuza

She's the hero we all need @tracy.kyakyo

Tracy Ahumuza turned personal tragedy, the loss of her newborn baby, Alyssa, into a mission to save lives. She founded ATTA Breastmilk Community in Uganda, creating a safe, inclusive system for mothers to donate breastmilk to support fragile newborns. Her work has provided life-saving milk to hundreds of babies, breaking barriers and building hope through love and resilience.
