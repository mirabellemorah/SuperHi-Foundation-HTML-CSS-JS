A love story so beautiful

The tale

In shadowed woods where sunlight dapples
A maiden fair with eyes of sapphires
Did meet a knight, his armor gleams
A love born 'midst of whispered dreams.
The moonlit stream, a silver thread
Reflected stars, a mystic bed
Where lovers met, their spirits entwined
In nature's grace, true love they find.
The rustling leaves, a lover's sigh
The distant owl, a lullaby
The world a stage, where magic reigned
And love's sweet song forever chained.

Him

My lady fair, with eyes of summer sky,
My heart, a captive, longs to be with thee.
I'll shield you ever from the world's cold sigh,
And in your gaze, my soul eternally.
Forever yours, my love, eternally.

Her

Your love, a beacon in the darkest night,
Warms my soul with its gentle, steady flame.
With you beside me, I will face the light,
And fear no shadows, nor the whispered name
Of danger, for your arms will keep me tame.
